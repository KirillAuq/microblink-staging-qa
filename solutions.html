About this audit

Full copy audit of the 16 solutions pages against the “Web Redesign - Solutions” doc. The pages follow a consistent template (Hero → Balancing/Challenge → Solution → How it Works → Key Benefits → Why Microblink → “See … in Action” CTA → FAQ). The body copy is largely a faithful match; the recurring problems are wrong/generic content in specific blocks (see systemic patterns). Expected template chrome (testimonials, blog feed, logo strips, footer, embedded form) is excluded from findings.

Systemic patterns

Issues that recur across pages — fixing the source/template once likely resolves most instances.
Generic closing CTA block
~8 pages
The bottom “See [Solution] in Action” CTA shows generic “See Identity Verification in Action” + “Designed for modern digital onboarding…” boilerplate instead of the page-specific approved heading/body. HIGH on Employment, KYA, AML/PEP; MED on IDV, KYC&AML, eKYC, Background Checks; body missing on BNPL.
Wrong-page content injected
6 pages
Specific sections show content from other pages/templates: Guest Check-in (Ecommerce paragraph), Payment Fraud (IDV hero + stray FAQ), GenAI (stray FAQ), Synthetic (wrong benefit body), Age Verification (all 5 benefits generic), Injection (BNPL fragment in heading + testimonial in CTA body).
Stray/duplicated FAQ
Payment Fraud, GenAI & Deepfake Fraud
Both pages carry the same injected FAQ — “Which document types and countries are supported…” paired with an unrelated on-device-privacy answer. Not in approved copy; remove.
Template CTAs & testimonials added
Most pages
“Explore Technology” / “Learn More” / “Try it Yourself” buttons and a 3-quote testimonial block (Instacart / Banco Azteca / Latin ID) are added site-wide. Mostly expected chrome, but some quotes are off-topic for the page (e.g., an engineer quote on BNPL).
“Why Microblink” → “Why Microblink?”
Many pages
Trailing question mark added to the section heading across solution pages — trivial, but inconsistent with the doc.
